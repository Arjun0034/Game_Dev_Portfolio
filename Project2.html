Project: Car Racing 3D

Project Description

I created "Car Racing 3D," a fun 3D racing game with help from a YouTube tutorial. I added a steering system for precise control and designed a menu for players to pick their favorite cars. I also improved the game by making opponent cars follow specific paths, added a pause menu for convenience, and expanded the car options by modifying their attributes. My project shows how I can enhance a game and make it more exciting, providing players with a diverse and enjoyable experience.
